About Us

We are the student chapter affiliated with both SIAM (the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics) and the IMA (Institute for Mathematics and its Applications) at the University of Bath.

Our committee of PhD students organises talks, socials, and conferences to bring together those passionate about applied mathematics and its real-world impact. Membership is open to all students at the University of Bath.
